Purpose

This website is a lightweight static developer page for your Google Play app listing. It is designed to host app-ads.txt at the root of a verified developer domain.

How it works

  • Google Play reads the developer website domain from your app listing.
  • It looks for /app-ads.txt on that domain.
  • The file must contain the authorized publisher record for AdMob/Google ads.

Next step

Deploy this site to your domain and ensure the file is publicly accessible at https://YOUR_DOMAIN/app-ads.txt.
