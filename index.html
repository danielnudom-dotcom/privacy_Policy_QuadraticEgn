Publish your app-ads.txt correctly

Host your developer site at the exact domain shown in Google Play, then place app-ads.txt at the root of that domain.

Important: Google Play checks the developer website domain, not your app package.

Required entry

google.com, pub-8527745383072875, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0

This file must be accessible at:

https://YOUR_DOMAIN/app-ads.txt

Verification steps

  1. Publish the site on your developer website domain.
  2. Verify the file is reachable in a browser.
  3. Use Google Play Console app-ads.txt crawler to check the file.

If you want, you can use GitHub Pages with a custom domain to host this site.
