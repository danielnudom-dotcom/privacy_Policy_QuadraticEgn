Effective Date: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Quadratic Equation App ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • User Input: Equations, coefficients, and mathematical expressions you enter into the App
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address and username
  • Feedback: Comments, suggestions, or support requests you submit

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, device ID, and hardware model
  • Usage Data: App features you use, timestamps, and interaction patterns
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and diagnostic information to improve app stability
  • Analytics: General usage statistics and app performance metrics

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, address, phone number) unless voluntarily provided
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or files
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT collect biometric data
  • We do NOT store your equations or calculations without your explicit consent

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Functionality: To provide, maintain, and improve the App's features
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and improve user experience
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud and security issues
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics and similar services to understand app usage
  • Crash Reporting: Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app stability
  • Cloud Services: Data storage and backup services

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental request.

4.3 Business Transfers

If the App or Company is sold, merged, or acquired, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4.4 No Sale of Data

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

5.1 COPPA Compliance

The Quadratic Equation App is designed for educational purposes and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If your target audience includes children under 13:

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do NOT use persistent identifiers or cross-app tracking
  • We do NOT share data with third parties for behavioral advertising
  • We do NOT allow behavioral or contextual advertising
  • We do NOT require collection of personal information for using the App
  • All collected data is used solely to support internal operations

5.2 Parental Consent

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete such information and notify the parent or guardian.

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Security Measures:
